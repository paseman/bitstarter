Blossom starts out simply, by allowing you to integrate together devices you already have. Just take a picture of the Devices face, and Blossom will transcribe and record the reading; allowing you to do diet planning with Scales you already own; Blood pressure monitoring with devices you've already purchased and will report and relate the readings you already take. But Blossom Diagnostics also has products which allow you to take readings on the go. Our probes can fit either on the back of your phone, allowing your phone to check your pulse by simply holding it, or in a specially designed bluetooth headset.

Blossom's' "on ramp" module is PulseSensor, which measures pulse rate. Blossom's Pulseoximeter probe performs full pulseoximetry, measuring both pulse and SpO2 (dissolved Blood Oxygen). Finally, Blossom's Hemometer measures two elements of a Blood Panel: Hemoglobin and Hematocrit. Whether you are a field physician needing to treat and triage patients rapidly, a clinic that can't wait for a 4 day lab turnaround or a blood bank needing to prequalify donors, Blossom has a cost effective solution for you.
